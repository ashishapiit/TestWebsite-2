STERILIZATION

Keeping patients and staff safe and healthy is the prime concern at our practice. Patients demand more information and assurances concerning infection control and would want to know the precautions that we take to prevent the spread of harmful bacterial and viral infections.

Our state of the art sterilization procedures ensure that our tools are cleaned, sterilized and hygienically stored in the most safe and efficient way possible to protect our patients and staff from cross-infection.

At Elite Dental Practice, our sterilization facilities are routinely monitored and certified by TEJAS LABS-in technical consultation with UNIVERSITY of LOUISVILLE, School of Dentistry through means of sterilization monitoring service and documentation of test reports on a routine basis.
