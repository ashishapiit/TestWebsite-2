Dr.Gautham
B.D.S. ( Bangalore university -1998 )
M.D.S. ( Rajiv gandhi university of health sciences - 2002)
Dr.Gautham received his post graduate qualification  in the field of Restorative Dentistry and Endodontics  , affiliated to  Rajiv Gandhi  university of health sciences , as a best outgoing student .

Prior to ELITE DENTAL PRACTICE , his experience in a group private practice for more than 10 years at WHITE HOUSE DENTAL CLINIC, in various fields of dentistry , along with  Dr.Shekar  ( Senior orthodontist  and ex- principal of KLE DENTAL COLLEGE , Bangalore  ) , has enforced his vision in providing unparralled patient care and offering the highest quality dentistry available today .

His specialisation interest lies in  root canal treatment , cosmetic dentistry , teeth whitening , minimal invasive dentistry and restorative dentistry including tooth veneers, crown and bridges.

He is currently working as a professor at the prestigious institution, BANGALORE INSTITUTE OF DENTAL SCIENCES, Bangalore where he is involved in conducting various research activities and training post -graduate students.He is also a member of the INDIAN ASSOCIATION of CONSERVATIVE DENTISTRY AND ENDODONTICS.
Dr Akshai Shetty
B.D.S; M.D.S
M Orth R.C.S (U.K);FCFO(T.W)
Dr Akshai Shetty has been in private practice as an orthodontist since completing his post-graduate program from RGUHS in 2001.He is a diplomat from the Royal College Of Surgeons Of Edinburgh and has also undergone special training in advanced orthodontics from CGMH University in Taiwan.

As an Orthodontist, he offers treatment ranging from straightening a single tooth to complex, surgical repositioning of jaws, Cleft and other craniofacial abnormalities. He has been on the forefront of recent technological advancements in orthodontics, along with traditional braces he is also a leader in the practice of lingual braces, Invisible aligners, implant assisted orthodontics and surgical orthodontics.

Presently working as a Professor in the Dept. of Orthodontics at R.V Dental College, Bangalore, Dr.Akshai Shetty is also a member of the Indian Orthodontic society. During his career he has been awarded with various prestigious awards.
Dr. Pritham N Shetty
MDS, MoMRCS (Glas)
Dr. Pritham Shetty is an oral & maxillofacial surgeon with varied experience in this field .He has specialized in the discipline of cleft lip palate and cranio-facial anomalies and is a consultant at the Bhagwan Mahaveer Jain Hospital, Bangalore, for “cleft palate centre’’.

He is currently working as a professor at the prestigious institution, BANGALORE INSTITUTE OF DENTAL SCIENCES, Bangalore where he is involved in training post-graduate students and conducting various research activities in implantology and special surgical procedures.
