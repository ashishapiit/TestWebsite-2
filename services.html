SERVICES PROVIDED

Your first visit to our dental office would fetch your needs through a detailed examination and proper treatment planning .

A preventive programme at our practice is a cooperative effort by the patient and the dentist to preserve the natural dentition and the supporting structures by preventing the onset , progress and recurrence of dental diseases and conditions .

We provide a complete comprehensive dental care with cleaning and polishing , education on proper oral hygiene and tips on proper brushing and flossing .

We take pride to incorporate the latest technology in digital radiography at our practice to enhance  diagnosis for accurate treatment planning and also to make you feel safe and deserve the best with minimal exposure to radiation .

We offer a wide range of services with the state of the art technology . Most of the services we excel in are associated with non-invasive procedures to restore your teeth and its supporting structures .

Our practice offers  smoking cessation advice to patients during routine check up. Advice is given on the effects  of smoking on oral and general health of a patient

OUR RANGE OF sSERVICES INCLUDE :

  • Restorative dentistry
  • Root canal therapy
  • Paediatric dentistry
  • Orthodontics for braces
  • Gum treatment
  • Dentures
  • Tooth extraction
  • Porcelain Veneers
  • Dental Implants
  • Crowns and Bridges
  • Laser assisted dentistry
  • Special children needs
  • Cleft lip and palate corrections
  • Cosmetic enhancements like ,teeth whitening , tooth jewellery ,smile makeovers and home whitening systems .

All our technique sensitive procedures are performed wisely with modern state of the art technology .  We work with precession for a high success rate .
